APFC Panels

Optimize Power Factor, Reduce Costs, Enhance Efficiency

Automatic Power Factor Correction

Our APFC panels automatically maintain optimal power factor, eliminating penalties from electricity providers while reducing overall energy consumption and improving equipment lifespan.

APFC Panel Features:

  • Microprocessor-based intelligent control systems
  • Real-time power factor monitoring and correction
  • Capacitor bank switching with step control
  • Harmonic filtering and protection
  • Digital display for all electrical parameters
  • Automatic/manual operation modes
  • Over-voltage and under-voltage protection
  • Temperature monitoring and control
  • Remote monitoring capability via SCADA
  • Custom panel design for specific requirements

Benefits of APFC Systems:

  • Eliminate power factor penalty charges (save 10-30%)
  • Reduce current drawn from supply, lowering cable losses
  • Increase available power capacity without upgrading supply
  • Extend equipment life by reducing electrical stress
  • Improve voltage regulation and system stability
  • Reduce transformer and generator KVA requirements
  • Lower maintenance costs for electrical equipment
