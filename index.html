Understanding the Results

  • Sound Level (dB): Measures loudness. 30 dB is whisper-quiet, 60 dB is normal conversation, 85+ dB can cause hearing damage
  • Peak Frequency: The dominant frequency in the sound, helps identify noise sources
  • Frequency Spectrum: Shows energy distribution across frequencies
  • Waveform: Visual representation of sound wave amplitude
