About This Tool

Our real-time audio analyzer uses advanced Web Audio API technology to provide professional-grade acoustic measurements directly in your browser. Perfect for acoustic assessments, noise level monitoring, and sound quality analysis.

  • Real-time FFT (Fast Fourier Transform) frequency analysis
  • A-weighted sound level measurements (dB(A)) calibrated for human hearing
  • Automatic background noise detection and tracking
  • Peak level recording for acoustic events
  • Visual frequency spectrum display with background noise reference
  • Works on any device with a microphone (desktop, tablet, mobile)
